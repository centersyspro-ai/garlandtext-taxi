About Us

Trusted transportation for over three decades

Founded in 1990 in Garland, Texas, Garland Metro Taxi has been providing reliable transportation services to the local community and surrounding areas for more than 30 years.

We pride ourselves on our punctuality, safety, and customer-focused approach. Our drivers are experienced professionals who know the Dallas-Fort Worth metroplex like the back of their hand.

Whether you need a quick ride across town, an airport transfer, or a scheduled appointment pickup, we're here to get you there safely and on time.
